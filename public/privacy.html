Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the “Color Picker & Mini-Games” MCP server (“the Service”) handles information. In short: the Service does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

What the Service does

The Service provides interactive mini apps (a color picker and several randomized games). Each tool takes a small, optional input — such as a hex color, a number of dice, or a list of wheel labels — and returns a result. Results are generated on the fly and are not tied to any identity.

Information we collect

None. The Service has no user accounts, no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking. It does not request or store names, email addresses, contacts, files, or any other personal information. The inputs you provide to a tool are processed only to produce that tool’s result and are not retained after the request completes.

Hosting and logs

The Service is hosted on a third-party cloud platform (e.g. Vercel). Like most web infrastructure, the hosting provider may automatically process standard, transient request metadata (such as IP address and timestamps) for operational, reliability, and security purposes. This data is handled under the hosting provider’s own privacy policy; the Service itself does not read, store, or use it.

The MCP host

You reach the Service through an MCP-enabled host (for example ChatGPT or Claude). Your conversation with that host, and how it transmits tool inputs and results, are governed by that host’s own privacy policy and terms.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
