Color Picker & Mini-Games

Pick colors and play

A single Model Context Protocol (MCP) server that adds six interactive mini apps to MCP-enabled hosts like ChatGPT and Claude — choose colors, roll dice, flip coins, draw cards, spin a wheel, and make simple randomized decisions with custom labels.

Color PickerHSV area, sliders, HEX/RGB/HSL, swatches
DiceRoll 1–5 dice, or a highest-total duel
Coin FlipHeads or tails, with a tally
Draw a CardShuffled 52-card deck
Spin the WheelEditable labels, add as many as you want
Decision DieCustom text faces (Yes/No/Maybe…)

Connect it

Add this server to your MCP-enabled host as a connector named IceBox, using its endpoint:

https://icebox.saurabh-yadav.me/mcp

Then ask, for example, “roll three dice” or “open a color picker starting from #2563eb”.

No account, no data collection. Every app runs locally in the host and generates its results on the fly. See the Privacy Policy for details.
